Change-Your-Life Chicken

Description:

A delicious and easy way to make chicken that will change your life!

Ingredients:

  • Chicken thighs with the bone and the skin
  • Two handfuls of vegetables per person
  • Olive oil, salt, and pepper

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees.
  2. Line a baking sheet with heavy duty foil.
  3. Cut your vegetables, and toss with olive oil, more salt than you think you need, and black pepper.
  4. Peel the skin back from the chicken.
  5. Pat the chicken skin dry with a wad of paper towels.
  6. Place the chicken skin side up directly on top of the vegetables.
  7. Bake at 500 degrees for 50 minutes.
